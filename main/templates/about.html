OUR VISION

FarmEase’s vision is to become the largest agricultural fair-trade platform, connecting farmers to buyers across Ireland.

OUR MISSION

FarmEase’s mission is to be THE reference for digital agricultural crop trading through a simple, transparent, and secure platform.

OUR FOCUS & EXPERIENCE

FarmEase is built by a team of experts in farming, procurement, and digital business. We aim to redistribute value in the agriculture supply chain through reliability, transparency, and agility.

KNOW-YOUR-CUSTOMER

KYC is used to verify the identity and origin of funds of users on FarmEase, preventing fraudulent activities and ensuring a trustworthy network of buyers and sellers.

KYC FOR SELLERS

Seller accounts are verified with ID, company registration documents, and bank proof to ensure legitimacy.

KYC FOR BUYERS

Buyer accounts are verified when withdrawing funds from the platform, ensuring safety and trust.

CERTIFIED B CORPORATION

FarmEase is committed to using business as a force for good, balancing purpose and profit while promoting sustainability and social responsibility in agriculture.
